Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India 🇮🇳

About

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known for his humility and vision, he inspired millions of students across the nation.

Achievements

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1998 - Played key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • 2002 - Became President of India
  • 2015 - Passed away while delivering a lecture

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
