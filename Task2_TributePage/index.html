Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India 🇮🇳

About

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He inspired millions with his vision, humility, and dedication to education.

Detailed Biography

Dr. A.P.J Abdul Kalam was born on 15 October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. Coming from a humble background, he sold newspapers as a child to support his family. He later studied aerospace engineering and joined DRDO and ISRO, where he played a key role in India's space and defense programs. He became the President of India in 2002 and was widely known as the "People's President". His life was dedicated to education, innovation, and inspiring youth.
